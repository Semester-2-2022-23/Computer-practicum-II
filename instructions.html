Instructions

The objective of the game is to eat the food. Every time the snake eats an apple, you score points. The more apples you eat, the higher your score will be. Move the snake towards the screen to eat the apples. The food will randomly appear on the screen, and each time the snake eats it, its length will increase.

Use the arrow keys to control the snake:

  • Up arrow: Move up
  • Down arrow: Move down
  • Left arrow: Move left
  • Right arrow: Move right

The game is over when the snake hits a wall or its own body. Your final score will be displayed on the screen. Press the "Restart" button on the screen to restart the game after it's over or by refreshing the page.

Have fun! The goal of the game is to have fun and challenge yourself to get a high score.
